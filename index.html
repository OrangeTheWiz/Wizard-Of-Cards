This is a website for the game Wizard-Of-Cards

The game is in very early beta and I do not even know if I am going to finish the game, or even work on the game after all.

Now, who am I? I am OrangeTheWiz on github, readyreid887 on itch.io.

Inspired by Slay the Spire, and OpenTTD, this is an isometric deck building keyboard based roguelike.

Or at least that is the idea for now.

As you might be able to see, this webpage's background is a screenshot of the game's isometric ground with some placeholder art.

You can find the placeholder art I used from Kenny at ​ https://kenney.nl/assets/isometric-blocks
